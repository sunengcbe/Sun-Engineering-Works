Our Thread Forms

At Sun Engineering Works, we offer a diverse variety of Thread Forms suitable for all applications with an ability to roll hardened and stainless steels.

the Solutions

We focus in rolling threads ranging from 2 TPI up to a maximum pitch of 6 inches, catering to a wide spectrum of industrial requirements. Our capabilities allow us to handle both fine and coarse thread profiles available with right- or left-handed threads. Supported by a collection of rolling dies, we are equipped to produce a variety of thread forms suitable for in-feed & thru-feed applications

Whitworth

Whitworth threads are widely used in specialty applications such as camera tripods and stage lighting equipment, where reliable fit and compatibility are essential. We offer both coarse (BSW) and fine (BSF) pitch options.

Acme

Acme threads are designed to generate smooth traversing motion in machine and tools. A wide variety of thread rolling dies available, and we can also arrange customized dies based on specific requirements.

Trapezodial

Trapezoidal threads feature a 15-degree flank angle, slightly higher than the 14.5-degree flank angle of the Acme form. Trapezoidal dies can be arranged and customized based on specific application requirements.
