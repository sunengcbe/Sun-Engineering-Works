Thread Forms

Five thread forms.
One standard.

If your drawing calls for it, we can probably roll it — standard or custom, left-hand or right.

We roll the thread forms below to standard specification, and we'll work with you on custom profiles when your application calls for something off-spec. Left-hand and right-hand threads, in-feed and thru-feed, from 2 TPI up to a 6" pitch.

Metric

ISO metric threads, the most common standard worldwide — designated by pitch in millimetres.

Unified

UNC, UNF and UNEF — the inch-series standard across North American industry.

Whitworth

The British standard inch thread, still specified on legacy and heavy-mechanical assemblies.

Acme

Stub and general-purpose Acme trapezoidal threads, built for power transmission and lead screws.

Trapezoidal

Metric trapezoidal (Tr) threads for motion and load-carrying lead screw applications.
