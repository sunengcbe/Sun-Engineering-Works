Our Story

A shop that treats
every thread like it's
load-bearing.

Most of what we do comes down to a single principle: a thread is only as good as the material it's left in. We cold-form threads instead of cutting them — pressing the material into shape rather than machining it away — because rolling leaves the grain structure intact and the thread stronger.

That's the difference between a thread that holds and one that doesn't. It's why we work across low carbon steel, stainless, alloy steel, aluminium, and copper, and why we'll roll to your drawing or ours — from a one-off repair to a production run.
