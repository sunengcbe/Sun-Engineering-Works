Knowledge

What Makes Rolled Threads Better?

Cut threads

are machined by removing material, disrupting the grain structure, which is ideal for large, custom, or brittle parts but produces weaker threads.

Rolled Threads

are created by cold-forming (pressing) material, making them stronger, more fatigue-resistant, and smoother due to continuous grain flow.
