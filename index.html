Sec. 02 — Why It Matters

What makes a rolled
thread better.

The difference sits inside the material, not on the surface.

Cut Thread

Material removed

Machined by cutting material away. That interrupts the grain structure at the root of the thread — the weak point where it matters most. Well suited to large, custom, or brittle parts.

Rolled Thread

Material displaced

Cold-formed by pressing material into shape. Grain flows continuously around the form — which is what makes rolled threads stronger, more fatigue-resistant, and smoother.
